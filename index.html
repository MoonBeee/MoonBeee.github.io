About Me

Born and raised in Halifax, I was ever curious. Fascinated by computers and technology I started creating websites at the age of ten. After 15 years of working in the restaurant industry I had learned a lot about teamwork and culinary, but I needed a change. I returned to school at NSCC in 2017 and started my programming journey.

Through my education I have had the pleasure of working on a few projects. I was part of Project Blackbird as a member of the development team. During Project Blackbird we created a web application to track students progression in their self directed studies.

For my NSCC Capstone Project I was part of team that developed a web application for the Halifax Higher Education Partnership. We created an application that will centralize information about events, programs, articulation programs, organizations, and available facilities that are offered by NSCC, Dalhousie, St. Mary's and other local post-secondary institutions.
